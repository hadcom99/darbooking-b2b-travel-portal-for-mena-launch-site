About DarBooking

DarBooking was founded to fix a problem enterprise bedbanks ignored: MENA travel agencies deserve modern tooling built for how they actually work — in Arabic, in local currency, and without months of contract negotiation.

We aggregate inventory from the world's leading suppliers into a single, RTL-native platform with wallet settlement and full margin control. Our mission is simple: help agencies book faster, protect their profit, and scale without the overhead of legacy integrations.

Today we're opening early access to agencies across Algeria, Tunisia, Morocco, and Egypt — with more markets to follow.
