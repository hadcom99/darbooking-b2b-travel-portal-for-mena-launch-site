About DarBooking

DarBooking is a MENA-first hotel booking platform that aggregates the world's largest bedbanks — Hotelbeds, TBO, and RateHawk — into one Arabic-native interface built for regional travel agencies.

We started because the tools available to agencies in Algeria, Tunisia, Morocco, and Egypt were built for someone else: English-only, contract-heavy, and margin-blind. We're changing that with local currency wallets, tiered markups, and one-day onboarding.

Our mission

Give every MENA travel agency instant, contract-free access to global inventory — priced, localized, and margined on their own terms.
